Bio

Harrison is a youth advocate dedicated to improving global health and well-being. He has contributed to a wide range of innovations in the field, ranging from inventing low-cost point-of-care diagnostics of foodborne pathogens to developing improved drug-delivery platforms for COVID-19 and Influenza vaccines.
